## ü§î What is Gomoku?

Gomoku, also known as Five in a Row, is a traditional board game where two players take turns placing their markers (X and O) on a 15x15 grid. The objective is to be the first player to get five of their markers in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

## üìñ How to Play

1. **Starting the Game**: 
   - Run the `main.py` file to start the game.
   - The game window will open, displaying the Gomoku board.

2. **Making a Move**:
   - Click on the grid where you want to place your marker.
   - The current player will alternate between X and O.

3. **Winning the Game**:
   - The game will automatically check for a winner after each move.
   - If a player wins, a message box will pop up announcing the winner.
   - The game will reset automatically, allowing you to play again.

4. **Current Player Status**:
   - The status label at the top of the window indicates which player's turn it is.
